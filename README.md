To call:
  Say "call", or enter the required amount to equalize bets.
To check:
  Say "check", or "0".
To raise/reraise:
  Enter the amount you'd like to raise in numerical form.
To fold:
  Say "fold".
To go all-in:
  Say "all in"
After completing a round, either say "exit" to exit the sim, or "play" to start another round.
